Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information: your name and email address when you register.
  • Expense data: amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data: basic information about how you interact with the app (e.g. pages visited), used only to improve the service.

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive financial account details.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service:

  • To display your expense history and summaries within the app.
  • To generate spending breakdowns and category reports for you.
  • To maintain and secure your account.
  • To communicate important service updates when necessary.

We do not use your data for advertising or sell it to any third party.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. However, no system is completely secure, and you provide your data at your own risk.

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Upon request, your data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly uses Google Fonts to render typography. This results in a request to Google's servers when you load the app. No personal or financial data is shared with Google through this integration.

We do not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data-broker services.

5. Cookies

Spendly uses only essential session cookies required for login and authentication. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us through the app. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.
